---
title: nu-card
type: element
description: A region element. It is used to visually and semantically separate a part of the content.
---

## Usage

A **default** card.

```html
<split/>
<nu-card>
  I'm a card.
</nu-card>
```

A **clear** card. A card with the shadow and without the border.

```html
<split/>
<nu-card clear>
  I'm a card.
</nu-card>
```
